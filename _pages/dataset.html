Right-Wing Extremist Mobilization in Germany

Kanol, E., & Knoesel, J. (2021). Right-Wing Extremist Mobilization in Germany. WZB Berlin Social Science Center, Version, 1(0).

More information

This is a dataset on right-wing extremist demonstrations that took place in Germany between 2005 and 2020. We used the German federal governmentâ€™s answers to brief parliamentary questions (Kleine Anfragen) tabled by the opposition left-wing party Die Linke to create this dataset. The dataset consists of 3290 observations and includes information on the location, date, number of participants, organizing actors, and the mottos of the right-wing extremist demonstrations.
